குறள் பழகு (Kural Pazhagu)

Tagline: An interactive, AI-powered Thirukkural learning app for students.

🎮 Just play the game, and Thirukkural Pazhagu will challenge you to arrange Kural words in the correct order, check your accuracy, and reward you with points to make learning fun and effective.

📖 Key Features:

  • 🧩 Arrange Words to Learn Kurals – Drag and drop scrambled words to form the correct Kural and understand its structure.
  • 🧠 Meaning-Based Exploration – Discover the meaning of each Kural in a simple, student-friendly way.
  • 🎯 Smart Scoring System – Get instant feedback and points based on word accuracy to track your progress.

🛡️ Safe and Secure

We organized our data into two JSON collections for efficient access and processing. The first collection, Adhigaram Data, includes the name and number of each Adhigaram. The second, Kural Data, contains each Kural’s Iyal, Paal, and Adhigaram, along with explanations from both Mu. Varadarajan and Solomon Pappaiya.
